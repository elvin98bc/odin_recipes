Recipe 2: Steamed Chicken Rice

Description

Steamed chicken rice is a classic Asian dish featuring succulent steamed chicken served atop fragrant jasmine rice, accompanied by flavorful sauces and garnishes for a harmonious and satisfying meal.

Ingredient

  • Chicken (1 whole, about 3-4 pounds)
  • Jasmine rice (2 cups)
  • Chicken broth or water (4 cups, for cooking rice)
  • Ginger (thumb-sized piece, sliced)
  • Garlic cloves (3-4, minced)
  • Soy sauce (3 tablespoons)
  • Sesame oil (1 tablespoon)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Cucumber slices and cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak 2 cups of jasmine rice for about 30 minutes.
  2. Clean and prepare a whole chicken, rubbing salt on the skin and inside the cavity.
  3. In a pot, combine the soaked rice, 4 cups of chicken broth or water, ginger slices, and minced garlic. Cook the rice until done.
  4. Meanwhile, steam the whole chicken over high heat for about 30-40 minutes or until fully cooked. You can use a steamer or improvise one.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, let it rest for a few minutes before chopping it into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Mix soy sauce and sesame oil, and drizzle this sauce over the chopped chicken.
  7. Serve the steamed chicken on a plate, surrounded by fragrant jasmine rice.
  8. Garnish with cucumber slices and fresh cilantro.
  9. Optionally, prepare a ginger garlic dipping sauce on the side for added flavor.
  10. Enjoy the wholesome and aromatic goodness of homemade steamed chicken rice!
